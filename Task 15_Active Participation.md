# Task 15 - Active Participation

## Event 1 — Do Drones Workshop, IISc Bengaluru

I participated in the **Do Drones Workshop** conducted by the UAV Laboratory,
Department of Aerospace Engineering, IISc Bengaluru in collaboration with
the HAL-IISc Skill Development Centre under the **SwaYaan initiative** by
MeitY.

**Duration:** 09 — 13 December 2025

**Course Content covered:**
- Introduction to Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs)
- Fundamentals of Aerodynamics, Multicopter and Fixed Wing Design
- Subsystems — Airframe, Structures, Avionics, Propulsion, Autopilot
and Control Systems
- Operations — BVLOS, UTM and AAM
- Practicals — Multirotor and Fixed Wing Assembly, Flight Simulator Training
- Ground Operations — Mission Planning and Supervised Flight Operations

**Faculty:**
- Dr. S N Omkar — Chief Research Scientist, Dept of Aerospace Engineering, IISc
- Dr. Subba Reddy B — Convener, HAL-IISc Skill Development Centre


---

## What I Learned

- How drones and UAVs work
- Aerodynamics of multicopter and fixed wing aircraft
- How to assemble a multirotor drone
- Flight simulator training
- Mission planning and flight operations
