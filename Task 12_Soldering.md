# Task 12 - Soldering

## What is Soldering?

Soldering is the process of joining two electronic components together
using a metal alloy called solder. A hot soldering iron melts the solder
wire which then cools and hardens creating a strong permanent electrical
connection between components.

---

## Equipment Used

- Soldering iron
- Solder wire (tin/lead alloy)
- Soldering wick (for removing excess solder)
- Flux (helps solder flow smoothly)
- Perf board
- Components (LED, resistor, wires)
